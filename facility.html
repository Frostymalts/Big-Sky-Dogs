Our facility is located on 10 acres. It has a large pond and a 2,000 square foot climate controlled kennel building. Each portion of the property is meticulously maintained and makes for a fun and exciting place for your dog to run and play. The grounds are also designed to mimic the different scenarios the dogs in the gun dog program will need to see to become successful hunters. The 9,000 square foot kennel yard is surrounded by 5 foot chain link fence and provides a safe area for your dog to run during group play. Our outdoor kennls are facing north and are covered by an overhang to ensure your dog is protected from the elements when relaxing outside.
